1) Depth First Traversal - In-Order (inOrder):
    Traverse the left subtree.
    Visit the node.
    Traverse the right subtree.

2) Depth First Traversal - Pre-Order (preOrder):
    Visit the node.
    Traverse the left subtree.
    Traverse the right subtree.

3) Depth First Traversal - Post-Order (postOrder):
    Traverse the left subtree.
    Traverse the right subtree.
    Visit the node.

4) Breadth First Traversal (breadthFirst):
    Use a queue to traverse nodes level by level, starting from the root.
